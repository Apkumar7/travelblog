Photography Tips

Photography Tips

  • Take pictures everyday
    Like any skill, practice makes perfect. With cellphone cameras evolving everyday, you don't need an SLR for great photos most of the time.

  • Always have a camera at the ready
    You never know when that great photo opportunity will walk right past you!

  • Know your gear
    If you are using an SLR, make sure to check your settings so that you have the correct ISO for your setting that day. Familiarize yourself with the options on your device. If you are using an Iphone, learn the different options like, night shot, long exposure, etc.

  • Have patience
    Any tourist attraction is bound to draw huge crowds. Even in a crowd, it is possible to get a perfect picture. Be patient and be ready to click when you see a break in the oncoming crowd. Some of the photos featured in this blog have been taken in crowded situations, but you can't tell that from the photos.

  • Plan your itinerary
    You want to plan to visit those picture perfect locations near or during sunrise or sunset to get the best photos. Don't be fooled, even if it is already dark outside, you can still take beautiful sunset photos with the Iphone's night shot mode. Some of the Centotaph pictures in the Rajasthan blog were taken when it was dark outside.
