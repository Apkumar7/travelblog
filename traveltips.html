Travel Tips

Travel Tips

  • Research ahead for a successful trip
    Start researching your destination months in advance. Trust me, it will make a difference. Apart from researching itineraries, make sure you research about the best time to visit, weather conditions, and the dress code if any.

  • Booking airline tickets
    Usually, the early bird gets the worm, however, when you're booking airline tickets, that might not be the case. There's usually a small window of time when the prices are more reasonable. Use websites like Google Flights that track prices and alert you when the prices drop. Beware as not all the websites offered by Google Flights are legitimate. Even after you book your tickets, keep tracking the prices so that you can rebook if needed.

  • Plan ahead
    International travel usually requires you to have a passport that is vaild at least 6 months after your travel date. Make sure to research any visa requirements.

  • Don't hesitate to interact with the locals
    The locals are usually more than happy to chat. They will also give you tips on the best local places to eat, stay and visit-information that you might not find on the internet.

  • Take time to understand the customs and culture
    This not only helps you know the "dos" and "don'ts" in the country, it will also add another dimension to your travel experience. Use a local guide when possible, they will highlight important places and history that otherwise might not be obvious.

  • Pack light
    Pack light for a stress-free trip, and you will want to save some space for souvenirs.
