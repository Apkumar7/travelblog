About






Who’s excited about 28 hour flights? I am! I love flying, looking for tickets, researching, planning, packing, and 11 hour flights–the whole nine yards. Why do I love it? It gets me places! Although I am guilty of asking "Are we there yet?", more often you will hear me asking "Where are we going next?". Travel is an experience that can immerse you in cultures and sights from far away lands. Even though photos don't always do justice, the pictures you take will bring lasting memories from your travels. I want to share my journey, passion for travel and photography. I hope you enjoy traveling along with me in my photo blog.
